GAZETTEER

How to use

This is meant to be a reference for the local, playable regions of the game.

Phaedra is where most of the game will take place for now.

You are free to create a character from one of these regions, or from somewhere else if you feeling like making something up.

Any info here is general knowledge that your characters probably know about the world.

If you decide to play as a non-human or a unique character, I might have more world details to share with you privately.

Phaedra

inspirations: ancient greece (brought up to iron age), basic-ass fantasy
A coastal, temperate region. Borders a great sea.

Notable Locations

Argope

A fishing town. Most notable for being the closest launching off point for the so-called Murder Island. Currently occupied by the Cult of Apollo, a regressive, militant cult dedicated to preserving purity and masculinity.

Murder Island

A large, misted island located near Argope that used to be connected by a land-bridge to the mainland. Rumors say that an ancient ruins lies at the heart of the tropical island, but few have survived to tell any stories about it. Rarely, Lizardfolk and Beastmen will leave the island, usually looking for mercenary work.

(Note: Lizardfolk and Beastmen are not playable yet, but they will be eventually.)

Camp Kourotrophos

The base of the Cult of Apollo

Peacock Point

A cove and old lighthouse, known for its peacock population. Borders the river that connects to Aktunis, a distant large city.

Pan's Forest

A small, magical forest in northeast Phaedra. Rumors say that it's proximity to a powerful leyline brings is closer to the realms of gods and fae.

Common Languages

Phaedren

The common language of the area.

Woldish

The primary language of Dolmenwood

Arkunite

The primary language of the closest large city, off to the west. The language of trade and mercantile.

Uncommon Languages

Thracian

Language of the isolationist peoples of the so-called "Murder Island". Its origin is not known. Related to Phaedren.

Old Thracian

A lost language, mostly known to scholars and archeologists.

Gnollish

The language of the Beast peoples. A common language for mercenaries to know.

Reptillian

The language of the lizardfolk, known by the lizardfolk living on "Murder Island".

Northern Covenant

inspirations: witches, Hekate, and ancient greek mysteries mostly

A cold, forest city-state north of Phaedra and west of Dolmenwood. Ruled by a monarchy, but it well-known to be controlled by the Church of Hekate, a group of mystics and witches dedicated to the mysteries of Hekate. The Northern Covenant keeps close ties to the Duchy of Brackenwold in the neighboring Dolmenwood and is known as an ally to any witch.
